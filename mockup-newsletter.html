✨ Resin Workshop - Create Your Own Masterpiece

Get creative with resin! In this hands-on workshop, design and pour your own custom resin trays, bud vases, or coaster sets — perfect for gifting or decorating your home.

Learn how to mix, tint, and pour resin safely while experimenting with colors, metallics, botanicals, and glitter to create one-of-a-kind pieces that reflect your personal style.

🎨 What You'll Learn:

Step-by-step guidance from start to finish. All materials provided. Your piece will cure overnight and be ready for pickup within a few days.

No experience needed!
